# Reto SSH 1 — Enter the PQ Tunnel

A secure operations team has migrated one SSH gateway to a post-quantum-aware transport policy.

Your mission is to prove that:
- a classical SSH key exchange no longer works,
- the required hybrid PQC key exchange does work,
- and only then retrieve the mission token.

You are not expected to attack the host. You are expected to understand the SSH transport negotiation.
