# Rubric

## 0–4 points: Not yet sufficient
- Cannot explain TLS roles.
- No evidence of understanding the handshake.
- No live progress.

## 5–6 points: Basic understanding
- Can describe TLS in broad terms.
- Knows that certificates are not the whole story.
- Needs heavy support to proceed.

## 7–8 points: Competent
- Explains handshake vs certificate role.
- Uses the browser shell productively.
- Recovers the token.

## 9–10 points: Strong performance
- Recovers the token cleanly.
- Explains FIPS 203 / 204 / 205 correctly.
- Explains why hybrid TLS matters in PQ transition.
