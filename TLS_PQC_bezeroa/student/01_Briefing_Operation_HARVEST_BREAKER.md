# Operation HARVEST BREAKER — The Quantum Gate

## Mission Briefing
An internal research relay known as **The Quantum Gate** is suspected of running an experimental transport-security upgrade.

Your team has been granted access to a **controlled browser shell** connected to the relay’s internal segment.

Your mission is to recover the **mission token** from the gateway.

You are **not** expected to break the server.
You are expected to understand **how the gateway is defending its traffic**.

## Objective
Recover the token in the format:
```text
FLAG{...}
```

## Rules of engagement
- Use only the provided browser shell.
- No brute force.
- No denial of service.
- No attempts to escape the environment.
- This is a protocol investigation challenge.

## Atmosphere / story hook
The relay was built to resist **harvest now, decrypt later** surveillance. The engineers hid one mission token somewhere in the trust path so that only analysts who truly understand the upgrade can recover it.

Find it.
